Our Story

Founded in 2017, Dalton's Burger Spot has been dedicated to providing the community with high-quality meals made from the finest ingredients. To spread happiness through food and put smiles on all of our guests face. Being an establishment that is notorious for the amazing environment and delicious food if always our main goal.
